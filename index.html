What's the motivation?

Retailers of various industries struggle in an attempt to increase customer loyalty. The KABOOLA app offer digital receipts from the POS (Point of Sale) straight to the consumer's mobile, with personalized coupons on the receipt, to increase customer loyalty without any customer loyalty program.

What do we do?

We connect to retailers, and provide them a simple digital receipts mechanism. With KABOOLA, consumers receive their receipts immediately and effortlessly to the KABOOLA app, which concentrate all of their payments, no matter if performed by credit card or cash.

What about the shoppers?

Shoppers can easily browse, sort, and categorize their receipts, get insights about their consumer behavior, and receive on-receipt coupons they will want to use.

What makes us special?

By cooperating with a variety of retailers, we are able to apply high level learning algorithms that provide shoppers with personalized coupons exactly at the right moment, ensuring they will return to the store.
